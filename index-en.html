About me

I combine human intuition and AI-augmented workflow to accelerate design end-to-end — qual/quant data, research, full-stack UX/UI, from workshops to modern handoffs (yes, including MCP, Claude CLI, and good old Figma).

My GitHub is full of green squares because I actually vibecode. MRR, LTV, and CRO aren’t buzzwords — they’re part of how I think about design, because a product has to work for the people using it and for the people keeping it alive.

I’ve made it this far considering I started designing digital products when Adobe XD was still considered the “cutting-edge” tool. If nothing else, it taught me to adapt — whether it’s digital luxury for Panerai, banking products for UniCredit, or fast vibecoding in startup trenches.
